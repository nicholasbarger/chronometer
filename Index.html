Chronometer Date and Datetime Picker

The goal of this control is to allow accurate picking of dates and datetimes which incorporate timezone functionality.

The control is dependent on jQuery and moment.js.

The control must work in IE9+, Firefox, Safari, and Chrome. It also must contain wrappers for Knockout.js and Angular.js.

Samples

The following are a list of samples demonstrating the capabilities of the Chronometer Date and Datetime Picker:

Simple Date

Pick a date in the following format: YYYY-MM-DD. The end output should not have any time or timezone information.

Simple Datetime

Pick a date and time in the following format: YYYY-MM-DD as well as a provided timezone. The timezone indicates the timezone for the entry and should not automatically recalculate the value of the currently expressed date/time.
